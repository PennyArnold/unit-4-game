Crystal Collector Game

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. Click on a crystal to add points to your total score. Win the game by clicking the crystals to match the random number. Lose the game when you click a crystal and the number goes over the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
For each new game, the random number changes and the value of each crystal changes.
Good luck!
